Galaxy Wave is a top-down, space survival shooter I built as an experiment to see what I could build by primarily just "vibe coding". I even used AI for the music and sound effects! It uses a combination of the [Phaser game engine](https://phaser.io/) for the game interactivity, and React for the UI overlay. I'd like to develop it further someday when I have some more time.

Battle waves of enemies, earn coins, and upgrade your ship between waves. The controls are very simple. Your weapons fire automatically on a set interval, and you can use the WASD or arrow keys to turn and apply thrust to the ship.

How high of a score can you get?
